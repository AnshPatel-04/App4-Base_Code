Hives or Rash?

There is a common misconception that hives and rashes are interchangeable terms, but this is not entirely true. Hives are actually a specific form of rash, but not all rashes are a result of hives. To properly address skin concerns, its crucial to distinguish between rashes caused by hives and those caused by other factors.
